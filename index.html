Property Owner: All-Pro Auto Reconditioning, LLC

Property Name: Tiki House

Form Type: Guest Liability Waiver & Acknowledgment

This Guest Waiver Form ("Waiver") shall be executed by each Guest prior to entering the Property. By signing below, the Guest acknowledges and agrees to all terms set forth herein as a condition of access.

Defined Terms

As used in this Waiver, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

"Approved User" means the individual authorized by Ownership to use the Property for the stay during which this Waiver is executed.
"Company" means All-Pro Auto Reconditioning, LLC.
"Guest" means the individual executing this Waiver, who is accessing the Property as a visitor in the company of an Approved User.
"Ownership" means the principals and executive leadership of the Company.
"Pet" means any animal brought to the Property by the Approved User or a Guest.
"Property" means the Company-owned residential property known as the Tiki House.
"Use Policy" means the Tiki House Use Policy & Agreement, the governing document applicable to Approved Users, to which this Waiver is a companion.
"Watercraft" means any boat, personal watercraft, kayak, paddleboard, or other vessel associated with or accessible through the Property.
"Waiver" means this Tiki House Guest Waiver Form in its entirety.

1. Guest Acknowledgment

  • The Guest is accessing the Property solely as a visitor accompanying an Approved User and has no independent right of access.
  • The Guest understands that the Property is a Company-owned asset and that all access is a privilege subject to the Use Policy and this Waiver.
  • The Guest agrees to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with the Use Policy and to follow all reasonable directions of the Approved User and Ownership.

2. Property Rules & Care Standards

As a condition of access, the Guest shall comply with the following rules at all times:

  • No glitter, confetti, beads, or similar materials anywhere on the Property.
  • No taping, pinning, nailing, or fastening anything to walls, ceilings, doors, furniture, or fixtures.
  • No food shall be left behind in any area of the Property, including the refrigerator, counters, or exterior spaces.
  • No personal belongings shall be left behind upon departure.
  • No furniture shall be rearranged or the layout of the Property altered.
  • No permanent or semi-permanent modifications to the Property are permitted.
  • The Master Bedroom shall not be used unless Ownership has granted written approval for that specific stay.

3. Prohibited Uses

The following are strictly prohibited without exception:

  • Parties or events exceeding 20 persons in total.
  • Commercial activities of any kind.
  • Inviting or admitting individuals not authorized by Ownership.
  • Any activity that creates excessive liability risk, noise complaints after 11PM, or undue exposure of the Property.

4. Smoking Policy

  • Smoking of any kind is strictly prohibited inside the Property at all times, including cigarettes, cigars, vapes, e-cigarettes, and cannabis products.
  • Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas. All materials shall be fully extinguished and deposited in proper receptacles.
  • Any evidence of indoor smoking shall result in charges and remediation costs billed through the Approved User.

5. Prohibited Items

  • The Guest shall not leave any contraband, controlled substances, drugs, or drug paraphernalia — whether legal or illegal — on the Property at any time.
  • The Guest shall ensure all such items are removed from the Property upon departure. Violations may result in financial liability and removal from the Property.

6. Watercraft

  • Use of Watercraft is subject to advance written approval by Ownership and is not guaranteed as part of Property access.
  • The Guest shall use all required safety equipment, including life jackets where applicable.
  • All children shall wear life jackets at all times while on Watercraft or docks.
  • No Watercraft shall be operated by the Guest under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any impairing substance.
  • The Company assumes no liability for injuries, accidents, or losses related to Watercraft use. All risk is assumed by the Guest and the Approved User.

7. Financial Responsibility

  • The Guest acknowledges that any damage caused by the Guest to the Property, its contents, any Watercraft, or pool and hot tub equipment may result in financial liability to the Approved User, and the Guest agrees to cooperate fully in any damage assessment.
  • Violations of this Waiver may result in the Guest's removal from the Property and the potential loss of future access for the Approved User.

8. Pets

  • Pets are not permitted on the Property unless the Approved User has received prior written approval from Ownership for the specific stay.
  • Approved pets are not permitted on furniture, bedding, or in the Master Bedroom under any circumstances.
  • Any damage caused by a Pet brought by the Guest shall be the financial responsibility of the Approved User.

9. Occupancy & Vehicles

  • The maximum overnight occupancy of the Property is limited per the Use Policy. The Guest shall not admit or invite additional individuals beyond that limit.
  • All vehicles shall be parked in designated areas only. Boats on trailers, RVs, and oversized vehicles require prior written approval from Ownership.

10. Pool & Hot Tub

  • Pool and hot tub use is at the Guest's sole risk. The Company assumes no liability for any injury, accident, or loss arising from use.
  • Pool and hot tub hours are 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Glass containers are prohibited in and around these areas at all times.
  • Children shall be supervised by a responsible adult at all times while in or near the pool or hot tub.
  • The Guest shall not tamper with or add chemicals to the pool or hot tub system.

11. Emergency Protocols

  • In the event of a Property emergency, the Guest shall notify the Approved User immediately so that Ownership can be contacted.
  • The Guest shall not authorize repair services or take actions affecting Property systems without direction from the Approved User or Ownership.

12. Assumption of Risk, Release & Indemnification

  • The Guest enters and uses the Property — including all indoor areas, outdoor areas, water access points, docks, recreational features, and any Watercraft — voluntarily and at their own sole risk.
  • The Guest accepts the Property in its current, as-is condition and assumes all risk associated with its physical features, layout, recreational amenities, and any pre-existing conditions, whether known or unknown.
  • The Company shall not be liable for any injury, illness, death, loss, or damage sustained by the Guest arising from use of the Property, including claims arising from the Company's own negligence, regardless of cause, except in cases of the Company's gross negligence or willful misconduct.
  • The Company bears no liability for any injury, incident, or damage in which the voluntary consumption of alcohol, cannabis, or any impairing substance by the Guest was a contributing factor.
  • The Guest hereby releases, discharges, and holds harmless the Company, Ownership, and their respective officers, agents, and representatives from any and all claims, demands, losses, and liabilities of any kind arising out of or relating to the Guest's presence at or use of the Property.
  • The Guest agrees to indemnify and defend the Company and Ownership against any claim, loss, damage, or expense (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from the Guest's conduct, negligence, or violation of this Waiver while on or about the Property.

13. Severability

If any provision of this Waiver is found to be invalid, unenforceable, or contrary to applicable law, that provision shall be modified to the minimum extent necessary to make it enforceable, or severed if modification is not possible. The remaining provisions of this Waiver shall continue in full force and effect.
